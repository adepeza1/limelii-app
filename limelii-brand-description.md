# Limelii — Brand Description

**Limelii** is a hyper-local experience discovery app — your personal guide to the best things to do in your city.

It helps users find, save, and plan experiences (restaurants, bars, events, hidden gems) filtered by vibe, neighborhood, budget, and occasion — powered by AI itinerary building and social sharing.

---

## What Makes It Different

- **Vibe-first discovery** — not just categories, but moods (*Date Night, Rooftop Drinks, Fresh Air, Night Out*)
- **AI itinerary builder** — an assistant that builds personalized itineraries from plain-English prompts
- **Hyper-local filtering** — neighborhood-level discovery, not just city-wide
- **Social curation** — save and share collections with friends
- **Occasion-based** — built around real moments: date nights, group outings, visiting friends, solo exploration
